# sales-insights-of-a-company
This excel file helps you to know about the various performance measures of a company like customer performance, market performance, and the profit and loss statement.... which are going to help the organization to take key measures.

**Problem statement**

AtliQ hardwares is a company where it manufactures and sell  hardware items like pc's , Mouse, keyboards etc..... They have customers all over the world like croma, Best buy and online platforms like amazon and flipkart and have their own store as well for direct selling . They found that their performance is decreasing day by day. They want to generate reports on where they are lacking behind. They also want to look in to their  profit and loss statements as well.  They were interested to generate reports in excel.

**Solution**

Sales director of AtliQ hardware, decided to build excel reports for converting all the raw data in to visual representation to make data driven decisions. So hired an excel expert to solve this problem..

**Steps followed in this project**

1. Performed a high level analysis of data in excel which has given to us in csv format to get better understanding over the data.
2. The csv  files are imported in to Power query for performing the data cleaning and removing the unwanted columns and loaded as a connection and added this data in to the data model.
3. From the data model created some of the DAX measures like net sales 19, net sales 20 etc... using measures in power pivot and also some of the measures which are not part of the pivot table.
4. Generated the reports just like drag and drop
5. Also converted the net sales in to millions and to the one decimal point for the better looking.
6. After the initial report reviewed by the stakeholders, made changes to the report according to the review commands.
